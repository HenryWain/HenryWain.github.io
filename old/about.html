About

I have lived in Redcar & Cleveland my whole life, I am involved in several local groups and I am extremely passionate about doing the best job for our under 18s.

Key Points

Since my election, I have always prioritised 4 Key Points for a fairer, more equal, society:
• Better access to mental health services
• Climate action now not later
• The importance of Education, encouraging young people to stay in school.
• Keep our area tidy, and have pride in it, encouraging people to pick up litter through social media campaigns.
